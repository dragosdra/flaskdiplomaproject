Ilford XP2 Super: Versatile Monochrome with Modern Ease

  • Ilford XP2 Super is a unique black and white film stock known for its convenience and versatility. Unlike traditional black and white films, XP2 Super is a chromogenic film that can be processed using the convenient C41 color process. This makes it compatible with most color labs, offering monochrome enthusiasts a simplified development process. With its fine grain, sharpness, and excellent tonal range, XP2 Super is an excellent choice for photographers who appreciate the classic look of black and white, coupled with the ease of modern processing.
